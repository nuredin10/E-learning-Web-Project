About Us

D.A Packaging is a main contributor in environmental sustainability by playing a major role in changing the face of Addis Ababa by collaborating with waste paper collectors to collect and covert waste papers to useful resources. As a recycler, D.A Packaging is immensely contributing to the country’s socio-economic development by substituting import and saving the country’s foreign currency. It also creates employment opportunity in the zone and the surroundings by direct employment and giving chance to participate in waste paper collection.
